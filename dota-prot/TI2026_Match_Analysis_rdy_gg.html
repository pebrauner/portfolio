KEY INSIGHT NAVI's Anti-Mage + Axe core pairing is a deliberate counter-composition. AM's mana burn pairs perfectly with Axe's Berserker's Call — forcing Heroic's mana-heavy Invoker into impossible situations. GOTTHEJUICE's Invoker mirror was the surprise last-pick that Heroic had no ban left to answer.

PIVOTAL Heroic's decision to pick Phantom Assassin after NAVI first-picked AM was a calculated gamble. PA's Blur evasion naturally sidesteps AM's Mana Break, creating a genuine carry matchup. However, NAVI's draft around PA's weaknesses — Crystal Maiden's Freezing Field, Axe's Culling Blade — ultimately proved decisive.
